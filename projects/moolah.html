Problem Space

My roommates and I share a lot of expenses, and passing the same money back and forth between us is inefficient.

What It Does

A money-splitting bot which works similarly to the Splitwise app by allowing any group of people in a shared Discord server to share expenses, reducing daily money transfer down to a few transactions however often you want. Moolah does not require any new sign-ups or app downloads, working locally in your Discord server.

Project Path

  1. Started off as "RoomieBot": a rudimentary Discord bot that worked only in our server with our initials. A wrong input would probably break the bot.
  2. Discord slash commands were released, and I realized I could make something much better and more versatile.
  3. Reworked the entire bot, but kept the base functionality of sharing transactions between a group of people.
  4. Utilized Discord's direct messaging to allow for personal transaction tracking and budgeting, and providing exporting capabilities to allow for integration with other budget trackers.
